Attendance Generator

Welcome to the online Attendance Generator, made by Mohit Chahal.

To use this web-app, you'll have to follow the following setps:

  1. Upload/attach your data file with names of all the students, along with their roll numbers and related information you want printed in the output attendance
  2. Once your list is rendered on screen, click on P or A to mark their present or absent respectively.
  3. After having marked the attendance for the students, click on the Export button to choose your desired output format and download it.
